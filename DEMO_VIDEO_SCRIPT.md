## üí° PRO TIPS

### Voiceover Best Practices
1. **Warm up your voice** before recording
2. **Drink water** but not right before speaking (avoid mouth sounds)
3. **Stand up** while recording (better voice projection)
4. **Smile** while speaking (it changes your tone positively)
5. **Re-record** sections that don't feel right
6. **Be enthusiastic** but not over the top

### Visual Best Practices
1. **Slow down mouse movements** (easier to follow)
2. **Pause on important info** (give viewers time to read)
3. **Use zoom/highlight** for small text
4. **Show loading states** briefly (proves it's live)
5. **Smooth transitions** between sections
6. **Consistent pacing** throughout

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ‚ùå Moving mouse too quickly
- ‚ùå Clicking before explaining what you're clicking
- ‚ùå Mumbling or speaking too fast
- ‚ùå Long silent pauses
- ‚ùå Browser notification interruptions
- ‚ùå Forgetting to show key features
- ‚ùå Making video too long (lose viewer attention)
